    Seafood Stew

    Main dishes
    🍽

    Ingredients

    • ¼ cup olive oil
    • ¼ cup flour
    • 2 medium celery stalks, diced
    • 1 medium green pepper, diced
    • 3 cloves garlic, minced
    • 1 tbsp curry powder
    • 1 28-ounce can plum tomatoes, undrained
    • ¼ cup water
    • 2 tsp. sugar
    • 1 ½ tsp kosher salt
    • 1/8-1/4 tsp red pepper flakes
    • 1 pound white fish (flounder, sole, orange roughy)
    • 1 pound uncooked medium or large shrimp, shelled and deveined
    • Fresh parsley, chopped

    Instructions

    1. 1 Heat oil in large pot over medium heat. Stir in flour, cook, stirring constantly until flour is dark brown – about 10 minutes. Add celery, green pepper, onion, and garlic. Cook until vegetables are lightly browned and tender, about 10-15 minutes. Stir in curry powder, cook 1 minute. Add tomatoes, water, sugar, red pepper flakes and salt. Heat to a boil, then reduce heat to low and keep warm. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Cut fish into bite-sized chunks. Pour sauce into 2-quart casserole dish, arrange fish and shrimp in sauce. Bake casserole, uncovered 30-35 minutes. Sprinkle with fresh parsley. Serve with good crusty bread to dip in stew.
