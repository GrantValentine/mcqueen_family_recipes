    Grandpa Frank Reuben Sandwiches

    Main dishes
    🍽

    Ingredients

    • Grandpa Frank loved a good Reuben sandwich. His favorite was on marble rye bread and toasted. While this is not Grandpa Frank’s recipe, it is my interpretation of a delicious Reuben that he would have loved. Best eaten while playing Monopoly and watching football– his favorite games.
    • Good-quality pastrami, sliced very thin (I like Boar’s Head brand)
    • Swiss cheese, sliced
    • Sauerkraut, drained
    • Russian Dressing (adapted from Joshua Weissman, recipe below)
    • Marble rye bread, sliced

    Instructions

    1. 1 Butter the outsides of the marble rye bread. On this inside, generously coat with the Russian dressing. Place sliced Swiss cheese next to the dressing on all bread slices (the cheese on both bread slices serves as the glue to hold the sandwich together). Top one slice with drained sauerkraut. Stack a generous amount of pastrami on top of the sauerkraut. Top with other slice of marble rye bread with dressing and Swiss cheese. Grill on both sides of sandwich until toasted. Serve with dill pickles and potato chips.
    2. 2 Russian Dressing:
    3. 3 1 cup (210 g.) mayonnaise
    4. 4 1/3 cup (96 g.) ketchup
    5. 5 2 tbsp (32 g.) Crystal hot sauce
    6. 6 1 tbsp. horseradish (fresh if possible, but not creamed horseradish)
    7. 7 2.5 tsp (15 g.) Worcestershire sauce
    8. 8 1 tsp. (3 g.) smoked paprika
    9. 9 2-3 Tbsp thinly sliced fresh chives – hope you are growing these in your backyard!
    10. 10 2 cloves garlic, minced
    11. 11 Salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
    12. 12 Mix all ingredients in a bowl. Refrigerate until ready to use.
