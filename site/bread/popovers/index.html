    Popovers (Regular or Gluten-free)

    Bread
    🍽

    Ingredients

    • 4 large eggs (room temp or warmed in shallow bowl of warm water for 10 minutes)
    • 1 ½ cups lukewarm milk
    • 1 ½ tsp salt
    • 1 ½ cups flour (if gluten free, use 1 cup 1:1 GF flour and ½ cup rice flour)
    • 3 tbsp melted butter
    • Place all ingredients except butter in a blender. Blend until well mixed. Add butter and blend a few seconds. Place in well oiled popover pan.
    • If baking in steam oven, bake at Convection Steam setting 425 degrees F for 20 minutes, then reduce to 325 degrees for 12 minutes.
    • If baking in regular oven, bake at 450 degrees F for 20 minutes, reduce heat to 350 degrees and bake 10-15 minutes more.
    • After removing from oven, immediately take out of the pan. Place a small slit in bottom or popovers to release steam to prevent them from collapsing. Serve immediately with good butter, jam, or honey.
