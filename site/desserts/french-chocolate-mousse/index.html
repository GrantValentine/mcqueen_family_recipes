    French Chocolate Mousse

    Desserts 8 ingredients 5 steps

    Ingredients

    • 6 ounces semisweet baking chocolate, chopped
    • 3 tablespoons unsalted butter
    • 3 eggs, separated
    • ½ teaspoon cream of tartar
    • ¼ cup plus 2 tablespoons sugar, divided
    • ½ cup heavy cream, cold
    • ½ teaspoon vanilla extract
    • Whipped cream and chocolate shavings, for garnish

    Instructions

    1. 1

      Melt chocolate and butter together in a heatproof bowl set over barely simmering water, stirring until smooth. Remove from heat and cool slightly. Whisk in egg yolks one at a time. Set aside.

    2. 2

      In a separate bowl, beat egg whites until foamy. Add cream of tartar and continue beating. Gradually add ¼ cup sugar and beat until stiff peaks form.

    3. 3

      In another bowl, whip cream until slightly thickened. Add remaining 2 tablespoons sugar and vanilla; whip to soft peaks.

    4. 4

      Fold egg whites gently into the chocolate mixture to lighten it, then fold in whipped cream just until combined.

    5. 5

      Divide into serving glasses, cover, and refrigerate several hours. Garnish with whipped cream and chocolate shavings before serving.
