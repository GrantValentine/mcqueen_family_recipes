    Fresh Strawberry Mini-Cupcakes with White Chocolate Frosting

    Desserts
    🍽

    Ingredients

    • (Makes 12-16 regular-sized cupcakes)
    • 1 stick unsalted butter, room temperature
    • 1 cup sugar
    • 2 eggs
    • 1 1/2 cup flour
    • 1 teaspoon baking powder
    • 1/2 teaspoon salt
    • 1/3 cup milk
    • 1 teaspoon vanilla
    • 1 1/2 cup fresh strawberries, chopped
    • 4 ounces white chocolate, chopped (do not use chips)
    • 12 ounces softened cream cheese
    • 1 1/2 cups powdered sugar, divided into 2 ¾ cup amounts
    • 1 cups chilled heavy whipping cream.

    Instructions

    1. 1 Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Insert cupcake liners into 40 mini-muffin cups. Cream butter and sugar in medium bowl for 3-5 minutes or until fluffy. Add eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition. Combine dry ingredients and add to creamed mixture, alternating with milk, and mix for 3 minutes. Fold in vanilla and strawberries. Fill each mini-muffin cup with a generous 1 tablespoon of batter. Bake for 16-18 minutes. Cool and Frost. (For regular size muffins, bake for 20 – 24 minutes)
    2. 2 White Chocolate Frosting:
    3. 3 Place white chocolate in top of double boiler set over barely simmering water. Stir just until chocolate is melted, smooth and just warm (white chocolate seizes easily - do not overheat). White chocolate can be melted in microwave, but watch very carefully, stirring every 20 seconds. Beat cream cheese in large bowl until fluffy. Beat in 3/4 cup sugar, then warm chocolate. In separate bowl, beat whipping cream until beginning to thicken. Beat in 3/4 cup powdered sugar until medium-firm peaks form. Fold into cream cheese mixture in 2 additions. Cover, chill in refrigerator for 1-2 hours or up to 4 days prior to using frosting. Frost cupcakes and top each with strawberry slice.
    4. 4 Hundreds of these were made as mini cupcakes for Katie and Patrick’s wedding reception.
