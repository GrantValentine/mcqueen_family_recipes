    Pumpkin Pie

    Desserts
    🍽

    Ingredients

    • 3 eggs
    • 1/3 cup sugar
    • 1/3 cup brown sugar
    • 2 cups canned pureed pumpkin
    • 1 tsp ground ginger
    • 1 ½ tsp cinnamon
    • ½ tsp each cloves, allspice, and cardamom
    • Pinch of salt
    • ¾ cup heavy cream
    • ¾ cup half-n-half
    • Unbaked piecrust

    Instructions

    1. 1 Preheat oven to 450 degrees F. Beat eggs and sugar together until light and fluffy. Stir in pumpkin, spices, and salt; mix thoroughly. Stir in cream and half-n-half.
    2. 2 Place piecrust in 9-inch pie tin. Pour in filling.
    3. 3 Bake at 450 degrees F for 8 minutes, then reduce heat to 325 degrees F and bake for another 40-45 minutes or until filling is just set.
    4. 4 Let cool and serve with freshly whipped cream.
    5. 5 Mark’s favorite pie that I make at Thanksgiving. Adapted from Silver Palate cookbook.
