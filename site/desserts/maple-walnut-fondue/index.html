    Maple Walnut Fondue

    Desserts
    🍽

    Ingredients

    • 12 ounces white chocolate, chopped fine
    • 1 cup cream
    • 2 tbsp pure maple syrup
    • ½ tsp pure maple extract
    • ½ tsp cinnamon
    • ½ tsp freshly grated nutmet
    • ¼ tsp cardamom
    • ½ cup chopped toasted walnuts

    Instructions

    1. 1 In a double boiler, heat white chocolate and cream on low heat, stirring constantly, until mixture is smooth and creamy. (White chocolate can easily burn.) Add maple syrup, maple extract, and spices. Stir in walnuts, transfer to fondue pot and keep warm over low heat. Serve with gingersnap cookies, cubed gingerbread, apples, pears, etc.
