    Carrot Cake

    Desserts
    🍽

    Ingredients

    • (from The Silver Palate Cookbook with adaptations by Julie)
    • 3 cups all-purpose flour
    • 3 cups sugar
    • 1 teaspoon salt
    • 1 tablespoon baking soda
    • 1 tablespoon ground cinnamon
    • 1 ½ cups corn oil
    • 4 large eggs, lightly beaten
    • 1 tablespoon pure vanilla extract
    • 1 ½ cups shelled walnuts, chopped
    • 1 ½ cups shredded coconut
    • 1 1/3 cups pureed cooked carrots (use baby food carrots)
    • ¾ cup drained crushed pineapple
    • Cream-Cheese Frosting (recipe follows)
    • 12 ounces cream cheese, at room temperature
    • 9 tablespoons butter, at room temperature
    • 4 ½ - 5 cups powdered sugar
    • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
    • juice of ½ lemon

    Instructions

    1. 1 Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Grease three 9-inch cake pans. Line bottom of pans with wax paper or parchment paper.
    2. 2 Sift dry ingredients into a bowl. Add oil, eggs and vanilla. Beat well. Fold in walnuts, coconut, carrots and pineapple.
    3. 3 Pour batter into the prepared pans. Bake for 30 – 35 minutes or until edges have pulled away from sides and a cake tester inserted in center comes out clean.
    4. 4 Cool on a cake rack for 3 hours. Frost with cream-cheese frosting.
    5. 5 Cream Cheese Frosting
    6. 6 Cream together cream cheese and butter in a mixing bowl.
    7. 7 Slowly sift in powdered sugar and continue beating until fully incorporated. Mixture should be free of lumps.
    8. 8 Stir in vanilla and lemon juice. Frost cake.
