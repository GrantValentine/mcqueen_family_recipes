    Aunt Lori’s Quinoa Radicchio Salad

    Salads
    🍽

    Notes & Tips

    • My sister gave me this salad recipe in 2019. I frequently crave this salad and yummy combinations of flavor. I made some minor adaptations to the original recipe.
    • 1 cup quinoa
    • ½ head radicchio, cored and thinly sliced in small pieces
    • ¼ cup balsamic vinegar
    • 2 Tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
    • 1/3 cup dried tart cherries
    • 1/3 cup pistachios
    • 3 Tbsp fresh flat-leaf parsley
    • ½ teaspoon flaked sea salt (e.g. Maldon)
    • ¼ tsp. freshly ground black pepper
    • Place 2 cups of water in a small saucepan with a pinch of salt. Heat to boiling. Rinse quinoa in a fine-mesh strainer under cold water, drain. Add quinoa to boiling water, stir, reduce heat to low. Cover saucepan and cook over low heat for about 15-18 minutes or until water is absorbed. Remove quinoa from heat, fluff with a fork, and let cool slightly. Transfer to a large bowl, fluff again with a fork and let cool for ~20 minutes.
    • Add all other ingredients. Stir well and serve immediately. Leftover salad keeps well for a day or two.
