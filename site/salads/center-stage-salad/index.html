    Center Stage Salad

    Salads
    🍽

    Ingredients

    • 1 head Boston lettuce, rinsed and spun dry
    • 1 head green leaf lettuce, rinsed and spun dry
    • 4–6 ounces Roquefort cheese, crumbled (or blue cheese or Stilton)
    • ½ red onion, thinly sliced
    • 3 scallions, thinly sliced
    • 4 slices bacon, cooked crisp and crumbled (optional)
    • Cayenne nuts (see recipe below)
    • Raspberry vinaigrette (see recipe below)
    • ½ cup olive oil
    • 3 tablespoons raspberry vinegar
    • 1 tablespoon minced shallots (optional)
    • ¼ teaspoon salt
    • ⅛ teaspoon white pepper

    Instructions

    1. 1 Tear lettuces into bite-size pieces and place in a large bowl. Chill. When ready to serve, add cheese, onion, scallions, bacon (if using), and cayenne nuts. Toss with vinaigrette and serve immediately.
    2. 2 Cayenne Nuts
    3. 3 ⅓ cup sugar
    4. 4 4 tablespoons butter
    5. 5 ¼ cup fresh orange juice
    6. 6 1½ teaspoons salt
    7. 7 1¼ teaspoons ground cinnamon
    8. 8 ¼–½ teaspoon cayenne pepper
    9. 9 ¼ teaspoon ground mace
    10. 10 1 pound pecan halves
    11. 11 Preheat oven to 250°F. Line a jelly roll pan with foil. In a heavy skillet over low heat, combine sugar, butter, orange juice, salt, cinnamon, cayenne, and mace. Stir until butter melts and sugar dissolves. Increase heat to medium, add pecans, and toss until coated. Spread nuts in a single layer on prepared pan. Bake for 1 hour, stirring every 15 minutes. Remove to rack and separate nuts with a fork. Cool completely. Store in an airtight container up to 5 days or freeze up to 1 month. Bring to room temperature before serving. If sticky, reheat on a foil-lined pan at 250°F until crisp.
    12. 12 Raspberry Vinaigrette
    13. 13 Combine all ingredients in a blender or jar and blend or shake until emulsified.
