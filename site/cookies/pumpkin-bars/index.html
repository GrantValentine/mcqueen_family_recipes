    Pumpkin Bars

    Cookies
    🍽

    Ingredients

    • Bars
    • 4 eggs
    • 1¼ cups vegetable oil
    • 2¼ cups sugar
    • 1 (16-ounce) can pumpkin purée
    • 2 cups flour
    • ½ teaspoon salt
    • 1½ teaspoons ground cinnamon
    • 1 teaspoon baking soda
    • 1 cup mini chocolate chips
    • Cream Cheese Frosting
    • 4 ounces cream cheese
    • 6 tablespoons butter
    • 1–2 tablespoons cream or evaporated milk
    • 2¼ cups powdered sugar
    • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
    • Chocolate Drizzle
    • ⅔ cup semisweet or dark chocolate chips
    • 4 tablespoons butter

    Instructions

    1. 1 Preheat oven to 350°F. Grease an 11 × 17-inch pan.
    2. 2 Beat eggs, oil, and sugar until smooth. Mix in pumpkin. Add flour, salt, cinnamon, and baking soda in separate bowl and mix well. Add dry ingredients to egg mixture and mix just until combined. Stir in mini chocolate chips.
    3. 3 Pour batter into pan and bake 20–30 minutes, until set. Cool completely.
    4. 4 For frosting, beat all frosting ingredients until smooth. Spread over cooled bars.
    5. 5 For drizzle, melt chocolate chips and butter together and drizzle over frosted bars.
    6. 6 One of our favorite fall cookies!
