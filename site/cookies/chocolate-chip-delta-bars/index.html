    Chocolate Chip Delta Bars

    Cookies
    🍽

    Ingredients

    • For bars:
    • 1 cup softened butter
    • ½ cup sugar
    • ½ cup light brown sugar
    • 2 egg yolks (slightly beaten with 1 tbsp water)
    • 1 tsp vanilla
    • 2 cups sifted flour
    • ¼ tsp baking soda
    • 1 tsp baking powder
    • ¼ tsp salt
    • 1 (12-oz) package semisweet chocolate chips
    • For topping:
    • 2 egg whites
    • 1 cup light brown sugar
    • 1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
    • 2. In a large mixing bowl, with an electric mixer on high speed, beat butter until creamy. Add sugars and beat well. Beat in egg yolks and vanilla extract.
    • 3. In a separate bowl, sift together flour, baking soda, baking powder, and salt. Add to creamed mixture. Dough will be stiff.
    • 4. Press dough flat onto a greased 10 by 14 inch sheet, cover with chocolate chips, and bake for 10 minutes.
    • 5. Reduce oven heat to 325 degrees. Make meringue topping: Beat egg whites until very stiff; add brown sugar and beat well.
    • 6. Spread meringue across top of dough (don’t spread all the way to the edges).
    • 7. Return to oven for 12-15 minutes or until meringue is very light brown and dough is set.
    • 8. Let cool; cut into bars.
    • The one cookie that my mom (Elly or Gma) used to make were called Delta Bars. The base was made with shortening and then topped with a meringue with brown sugar. This is an adaptation of her cookie.
