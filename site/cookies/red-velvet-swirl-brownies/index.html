    Red Velvet Swirl Brownies

    Cookies
    🍽

    Ingredients

    • Red Velvet Brownie Layer:
    • 1 stick unsalted butter
    • 1 cup sugar
    • 1 tsp. vanilla
    • ¼ cup cocoa
    • pinch salt
    • 1 tbsp red food coloring
    • 1 tsp vinegar
    • 2 eggs
    • ¾ c. flour
    • Cream Cheese Layer:
    • 8 oz. cream cheese, softened
    • ¼ C. sugar
    • 1 egg
    • 1/8 tsp vanilla

    Instructions

    1. 1 Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Butter an 8x8 inch pan and set aside.
    2. 2 Brownie layer: In a saucepan on medium heat melt the butter. Remove the butter to a large bowl and add the sugar, vanilla, cocoa powder, salt, food coloring, and vinegar. In that order, mixing between additions. Whisk the eggs in a small bowl and stir it into the cocoa mix. Fold in the flour until lightly combined. Pour the batter into the prepared baking pan, saving ¼ cup of the batter for the top.
    3. 3 Cream cheese layer: Blend together the cream cheese, sugar, egg, and vanilla in a medium bowl. Gently spread the cream cheese layer on top of the brownie batter in the pan. Dollop the remaining brownie batter over the cream cheese layer. Using a skewer or the tip of a knife, drag the tip through the cream cheese mixture to create a swirl pattern. Bake the brownies for 30 minutes. Remove to a cooling rack and let cool.
