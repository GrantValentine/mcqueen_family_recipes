    S’mores Ooey Gooey Bars

    18 whole graham crackers, or two cellophane wrapped stacks 1 1/2 sticks (12 tablespoons) unsalted butter, melted 1 pinch of kosher salt 1 14 ounce can (400g) sweetened condensed milk 1 1/2 cups (250g) semisweet chocolate chips 2/3 bag of large marshmallows, cut in half, 1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees. In a food processor, process graham crackers until finely ground. Stir in melted butter and salt. Press into a 13×9″ pan.  Bake for 5 minutes.  Remove from oven. 2. Sprinkle on chocolate chips. Drizzle can of condensed milk over graham crackers; follow with marshmallows. 3. Bake for 20 minutes more, until marshmallows are lightly browned.  (To brown marshmallows to “perfect” brown, place under broiler for a few seconds.) Let cool completely., I didn’t make these much at home, but often for church summer events. Super easy.…
